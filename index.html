I’m Abe — a proud collector of all the Hironos!


Each Hirono has a story.



LE PITIT

Le Pitit Hirono was small but bold. She lived in a cozy teacup house and dreamed of skies made of candy clouds. One morning, she noticed the wind had stopped dancing. She packed her crayons and set off to find the missing breeze.





SWEET MONKEY

Sweet Monkey Hirono was swinging from trees when he heard silence where laughter should be. He grabbed his banana-sling and filled his pockets with candy seeds, ready to bring joy back however he could.



CLOT

Clot Hirono lived in a tall tower of books. She saw strange patterns in the stars — something was off. With her ancient scrolls and quiet strength, she prepared a spell to protect Hironovia from whatever was coming.
